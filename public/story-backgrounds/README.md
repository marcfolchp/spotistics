# Story Background Images

Place your monthly background images here with the following naming convention:

- `january.jpg` or `january.png`
- `february.jpg` or `february.png`
- `march.jpg` or `march.png`
- `april.jpg` or `april.png`
- `may.jpg` or `may.png`
- `june.jpg` or `june.png`
- `july.jpg` or `july.png`
- `august.jpg` or `august.png`
- `september.jpg` or `september.png`
- `october.jpg` or `october.png`
- `november.jpg` or `november.png`
- `december.jpg` or `december.png`

The images should be optimized for Instagram story dimensions (1080x1920px recommended).
